About California

California, once home to diverse Native American cultures, saw Spanish colonization in the late 18th century. It became Mexican territory in 1821, then part of the United States in 1848. The Gold Rush in 1848 brought a massive influx of people, leading to statehood in 1850. California thrived, becoming a global economic and cultural powerhouse.
